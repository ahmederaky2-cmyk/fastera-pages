Fastera

Privacy

Fastera is designed to be free, private, simple, and ad-free.

Data

Fasting sessions, weight entries, waist entries, settings, and reminders are stored on device.

Accounts

Fastera does not require an account to use the app.

Advertising and tracking

Fastera does not include ads and does not use third-party ad tracking.

Permissions

Fastera requests only the permissions needed for optional features such as local notifications and Live Activities.

Sharing

Your data is not sold. Your data is not shared with advertisers.

Export

You can export your data locally from inside the app.
