Support

Fastera keeps support simple too.

What Fastera does

Tracks fasting sessions, body weight, waist measurements, progress, history, reminders, widgets, and Apple Watch support.

What Fastera does not do

No ads, no subscriptions, no calorie tracking, no meal logging, no social feed, and no cloud account requirement.

Troubleshooting

  • If reminders are missing, check Notification settings for Fastera.
  • If Live Activities are hidden, enable Live Updates inside Fastera Settings.
  • If the Apple Watch app is missing, make sure Automatic App Install is enabled in the Watch app.
